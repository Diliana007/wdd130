Featured Rafting Experiences

Colorado River Run

Experience the majesty of the Colorado River as it winds through breathtaking canyons. This two-day expedition offers a perfect blend of thrilling rapids and serene moments to take in the stunning scenery.

  • Duration: 2 days / 1 night
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Minimum Age: 12 years
  • Group Size: 6-12 people
  • Includes: Camping gear, meals, professional guide
$299 per person

Bear River Rapids

Challenge yourself with the intense rapids of Bear River. This advanced-level trip is designed for experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush. Navigate class IV and V rapids with our expert guides.

  • Duration: Full day (8 hours)
  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Minimum Age: 16 years
  • Group Size: 4-8 people
  • Includes: Safety gear, lunch, professional guide
$199 per person
Book Now

Smoky Mountain Challenge

Perfect for families and first-timers, this three-day adventure through the Smoky Mountains offers scenic views and manageable rapids. Enjoy campfire nights and wildlife spotting during this unforgettable journey.

  • Duration: 3 days / 2 nights
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Minimum Age: 8 years
  • Group Size: 8-16 people
  • Includes: All camping equipment, meals, professional guides
$399 per person
