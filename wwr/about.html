Our History

Founded in 1999 by river enthusiast John Waters, Splash White Water Rafting Co. has grown from a single raft operation to one of the premier rafting companies in the region. What started as a passion project has transformed into a thriving business that has introduced over 50,000 adventurers to the thrill of white water rafting.

Our experienced guides have navigated rivers across three continents and bring that wealth of knowledge to every trip. We pride ourselves on our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and creating unforgettable experiences for our guests.
